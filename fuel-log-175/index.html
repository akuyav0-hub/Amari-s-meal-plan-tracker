      The whole job

      Eat the four meals the Today screen shows you. Tap each one as you eat it. When the ring fills and says ROLL TIDE, you hit your protein. Nothing to count or remember.

      You don't plan — the app does

      The week is fixed. Tuesday and Friday lunch is tuna, everything else is chicken, and Saturday dinner is your cheat meal. Today always shows the right one for that day. You never decide.

      Training or rest day?

      Lifted, ran, or trained? Tap Training day. Took it easy? Tap Rest / lean. The meals update to show more or fewer carbs — the protein stays the same.

      Shopping — once a week

      Open the Shop tab, buy exactly what's listed, match the brand names. About $49 at Aldi. Pantry stuff (rice, oats, PB) only every couple weeks.

      Cooking the chicken

      Sunday: bake the whole pack — 400°F, ~25 min. Split it into ½-pound (8 oz) raw pieces before cooking. Each = 50g, one for lunch, one for dinner. Season half two different ways.

      The one rule

      Tight on money or appetite? Cut the carbs — rice, oats, potato. Never cut the protein. Chicken, eggs, tuna, yogurt — that's the part that matters.
